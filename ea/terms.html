2. Nature of the Protocol

CryptoNova Matrix V8 is a decentralized, autonomous smart contract system deployed on the Base blockchain. It operates without a central administrator, custodian, or intermediary. Once deployed, the core logic — including fee splits — is immutable and cannot be altered by any party.

The protocol implements a 7-tier BFS matrix compensation structure where participants pay an entry fee in USDC and receive payments from subsequent participants according to predefined on-chain rules. Each $10 entry at Tier 1 is split as: 20% referral bonus to direct sponsor, 5% override to deeper upline, 20% chain pay across 6 upline levels, 33% equalization pool, 15% to the StabilityFund (which backs the CNOVA floor price), 5% dev & ops, and 2% DAO treasury reserve. The CNOVA token is distributed as an in-protocol reward and is not a security or investment contract.
